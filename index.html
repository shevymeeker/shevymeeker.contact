What these apps are built for

Keep it simple, fast, and focused on the one job that matters.

  • No "startup toy" energy. Each app is built to replace an actual process you or your clients already run in the wild.
  • You can run everything in a browser. No signups, no passwords, no "almost an app" friction.
  • Every paid app ships with documentation and one clear way to get help if something breaks.
  • Free tools double as a portfolio. They show how you think about workflow, not just code.
  • Sales pages stay honest. No fake urgency. Just what it does, who it is for, and why it earns its price.
