Projects

Over the last several years, everything has turned into SaaS. Every tool is a subscription or an ad funnel. Everything needs a password. Everything has to load. Updates break things. Servers go down. You think you saved, but you didn't. You get to a job site and the one tool you need is useless because there's no signal.

There had to be a better way.

That's when I started building progressive web apps. They install on your device and work offline. No subscriptions. No passwords. No ads. No signal required. It works whether you're camping, on a road trip, in an oil field, in a client's crawl space. Even in outer space.

I solved my problems, let's see if I can solve yours.
