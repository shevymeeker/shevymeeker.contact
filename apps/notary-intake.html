Offline-first structure

Normalized Dexie.js schema with indexed lookups for client name and status; cache-first service worker keeps the intake shell working without signal.

  • Tables: clients, signings (clientId), documents (signingId, tags).
  • Multi-entry index on document tags for quick filtering.
  • Dexie queries: where("status").equalsIgnoreCase("scheduled").
  • jsPDF exports a receipt once a signing is marked complete.
