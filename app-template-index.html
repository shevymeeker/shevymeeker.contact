How you actually use this

Keep this anchored in daily reality, not "roadmap" fluff.

Step 1
Plug in your real inputs

Set up your common job types, baseline hourly rate, and any fixed fees. This happens once and can be edited as your numbers shift.

Step 2
Build an estimate in front of the client or same day

Use the app to add sections, line items, and notes. It does the math and guards your margins while you focus on scope.

Step 3
Export and reuse the structure

Save the estimate as a PDF or copy. Reuse the structure so the next quote takes half the time and looks twice as consistent.

Questions people usually ask

Swap these for the actual questions you get.

Will this work on my phone?
Yes. Everything runs in the browser and is tested on modern mobile devices. If your phone can handle basic web apps, it can handle this.
What happens if I want to change my pricing later?
You can edit the underlying rates and constants yourself. If you want deeper structural changes, that turns into either a small paid tweak or a custom build.
Do you keep any of my client data?
On the default setup, nothing is stored on a server. Data lives in your browser or your exports. If you want a hosted setup, we can talk about storage and privacy directly.
Can I resell or white-label this for others?
If you want reseller rights, that is a different conversation and a different price. For personal or business use, you can use it as much as you want.
